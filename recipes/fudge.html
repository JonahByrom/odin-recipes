Peanut Butter Fudge

Description

This is an extremely simple recipie for peanut butter fudge. There are barely any ingredients at all, and it tastes delicious!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup (2 sticks) of unsalted butter, plus more for greasing the pan
  • 1 cup smooth peanut butter (not natural)
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/4 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 3 cups confectioners' sugar, sifted

Steps

  1. Grease an 8-inch-square pan with butter. Line the bottom with parchment paper, then grease the paper.
  2. In a medium saucepan, combine the butter and peanut butter. Cook over medium-low heat, stirring oftenm until melted and smooth, 3 to 4 minutes. Do not let the mixture come to a simmer. Off the heat, stir in the vanilla and salt.
  3. Scrape the peanut butter mixture into a large bowl. Gradually add the confectioners' sugar until the sugar is fully incorporated after each addition. After all the sugar as been added, mix well with a rubber spatual until smooth.
  4. Transfer the mixture to the prepared pan and smooth the top. Cover with plastic wrap, pressing the plastic to the surface of the fudge. Chill for at least 2 hours, until set. Use a knife to cut the fudge away from the pan, then lift the parchment paper to remove it. Cut into 1-inch squares. Store fudge, covered, in the refrigerator for up to 5 days or freeze for up to 2 months.
