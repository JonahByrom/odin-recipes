Southern Macaroni and Cheese

Description

This is a simple recipe for southern-style macaroni and cheese. It's creamy, cheesy, and delicious!

Ingredients

  • Kosher salt and black pepper
  • 1pound elbow macaroni
  • 2 cups whole milk
  • 2 Large eggs
  • 4 cups shredded extra-sharp Cheddar ( ~ 16 ounces)
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter (1 stick), melted
  • 2 cups shredded Colby Jack ( ~ 8 ounces)
